Farm Tools and Equipment in Crop Production

Farm tools, implements, and equipment play very important role in crop production. Their availability makes the work much easier and faster. However, even if one may have the most sophisticated tools and implements, but does not know how to use them, they are useless. In order to do crop production successfully, one must have a good working knowledge of the tools, implements and equipment before using them.


Definition of Terms

Tool – Any device or implements used to carry out mechanical functions whether manually or by a machine.
Farm – An area of land and its buildings used under one management for growing crops and rearing animals.
Hand Tools – These are objects that are usually light and are used without the help of animals and machineries.
Farm Tools – These are used in land preparations.

Hand Tools

These are tools made of light materials and are used manually. Most of the hand tools are used in cultivating the area for small garden or in backyard gardening.

Axe

Axe is for cutting bigger size post.

Crowbar

Crowbar is used for digging big holes and for digging out big stones and stumps.

Grab Hoe

Grab-hoe is used for breaking hard topsoil and pulverizing soil.

Hand Cultivator

Hand cultivator is used for cultivating the garden plot by loosening the soil and removing weeds around the plant.

Hand Fork

Hand fork is used for inter row cultivation.

Hand Trowel

Hand Trowel is used for loosening the soil around the growing plants and putting small amount of manure fertilizer in the soil.

Knife

Knife is for cutting planting materials and for performing other operations in horticulture.

Light Hoe

Light hoe is used for loosening and leveling soil and digging out furrows for planting.

Pick Mattock

Pick-mattock is used for digging canals breaking hard topsoil and for digging up stones and tree stumps.

Pruning Shears

Pruning shears is for cutting branches of planting materials and unnecessary branches of plants.

Rake

Rake is used for cleaning ground and leveling topsoil.

Shovel

Shovel is used in removing trash, digging loose soil, moving soil from one place to another and for mixing soil media.

Sickle

Sickle is a hand-held agricultural tool with a variously curved blade typically .

Spade

Spade is used for removing trash or soil, digging canals or ditches, and mixing soil media.

Spading Fork

Spading Fork is used for loosening the soil, digging out crops and turning over the materials in a compost heap.

Sprayers

Sprayers is a hand-held agricultural tool with a variously curved blade typically used for cutting weeds.

Sprinklers

Springklers are used for watering seedlings and young plants.

Water Pails

Water pails are used for hauling water, manure and fetilizers.

Wheel Barrow

Wheel barrows are used for carrying heavy loads of dirt, rocks, etc.

Common Farm Equipments

These are machineries used in horticultural operations especially in vegetable production. They are used in land preparation and in transporting farm inputs and products. This equipment needs a highly skilled operator to use.

Four Wheel Tractor

Four-wheel tractor is used to pull disc plow and disc harrow in preparing much bigger area of land.

Hand Tractor

Hand tractor is used to pull a plow and harrow in preparing a large area of land..

Water Pumps

Water pumps are used to draw irrigation water from a source..
